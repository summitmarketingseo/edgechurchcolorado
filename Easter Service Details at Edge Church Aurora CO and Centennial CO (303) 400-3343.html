Easter Service Details at Edge Church Aurora CO and Centennial CO (303) 400-3343

Edge Church is excited to announce details about their upcoming easter services. Easter services represent one of the most important times of the year in the Christian calendar and the church is looking forward to welcoming friends old and new to enjoy the celebrations.
Whether people are looking for an Aurora church or Centennial church to attend, they can be sure of a warm and friendly welcome, and an uplifting and empowering worship service.
Edge Church Pastor, Pastor Ryan has a powerful message just for you, A New Hope. Hopelessness is a universal struggle. It’s easy to be discouraged. But the message of Easter is a message of hope! And God wants to move you from hope-LESS to being hope-FULL. Come Easter Sunday and discover new hope for your life!
People are invited to join the congregation for one of 2 identical worship experiences this Easter! They can expect a powerful time of worship and a practical message by Pastor Ryan. There is no need to dress up in your Sunday best because, at Edge, their motto is No Perfect People Allowed. Visitors will be warmly welcomed no matter where they are in their spiritual journey.
Kids from birth to 5th grade will experience a creative time of worship and Bible stories during each worship service. And for students in 6th to 8th grade, middle schoolers will have a blast in the Middle School Experience at 9:30 am in The Hangout.
Edge Church take a great deal of pride in providing a warm welcome to anyone visiting the church for the first time, so let them know in advance that you will be attending for a special welcome.
“Easter is one of the foundational stones of the Christian church,” said Pastor Ryan of Edge Church. “We are looking forward to gathering together as a family and providing people with hope for the future. Although I enjoy every part of the Christian Calendar, Easter has always been a special time of the year for me, and this year is no exception. I am looking forward to delivering my sermon, and hope that the congregation will take a lot of hope and inspiration from it.
Edge Church is a new church in Aurora / Centennial. It’s a place where you can come just as you are. You’ll discover an authentic community of faith where there are No Perfect People Allowed. Come see us this Sunday at 9:30 or 11:00 am!
